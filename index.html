OUR STORY

On a hot August 2012 day while on a Peter Pan bus to Cape Cod Piotr decided to check up on his online dating profile. He joined a month prior, had yet to go on a date, and had nothing but low expecations. The rest is history...

FUN FACTS

First Three Dates

PS1 MoMA, Pianos NYC, Manhattan Inn.

Hurricane Sandy

Piotr was stranded with Emily in Astoria for two weeks in October. This is probably what sealed it because they were still into each other.

Over the next few months...

Emily finished grad school while Piotr freelanced. They would see each other in the evenings or on the weekends. Piotr was basically the 5th roommate.

Moving out West

Piotr and Emily only had a ten day sublet lined up when they boarded their flight.

First to La Jolla

Emily got her first job as a school psychologist in San Diego. Emily loved La Jolla.

Piotr to LA

Piotr then got a job in LA. He lived with a friend and visited Emily on the weekends.

Emily joined Piotr

He was the one thing that Emily loved more than La Jolla...

SHE SAID YES

Two days after they moved to their new place Piotr proposed to Emily on June 28, 2015. The fact that Piotr wanted to marry Emily was no secret, they both designed the ring together using a family heirloom diamond. In fact, Piotr planned on proposing to Emily earlier in the month while on a trip to Palm Springs, but found out days before that the ring was not ready. He took this delay in stride even though Emily was pretty restless. In the end the proposal was perfect, in front of their pets in their new living room.



We're excited to start the next chapter of our lives together and can't wait to celebrate with you!
